    Services

    By Appointment

    A Bath & Trim includes a bath, blow dry & brush out, tidying up around the face & paws and nail trims.

    A Full Groom includes a bath, blow dry & brush, a full style, nail trim, and ear clean. An anal expression will be done upon request.

    For Paw Trim and Ear Cleaning call to check. 

    Walk-In

    No appointments are needed for Nail Trims.
